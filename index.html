SIX DIMENSIONS
Not technology skills — thinking skills that matter in every career.
DIMENSION 01
Prompt Engineering
Can the student communicate precisely with AI to get the best output? The sharper the instruction, the more powerful the tool — and this skill transfers to every job.
A journalist prompting AI to research a breaking story under deadline pressure.
DIMENSION 02
AI Safety & Ethics
Does the student understand responsibility, privacy, and the limits of ethical AI use? When AI causes harm, someone must be accountable — and understanding that is a critical life skill.
A lawyer understanding AI evidence admissibility and data privacy in court.
DIMENSION 03
Spotting Hallucinations & Bias
Can the student identify when AI is wrong, misleading, or systematically unfair? Confidence in AI output is not the same as accuracy — and the difference matters enormously.
A doctor questioning an AI diagnostic before it becomes a treatment decision.
DIMENSION 04
AI in the Real World
Does the student see how AI is already reshaping industries and jobs? Seven types of AI — and the specific type changing their chosen field right now, today.
A farmer understanding AI-driven crop prediction, pest detection, and market tools.
DIMENSION 05
Creative Use of AI
Can the student use AI as a genuine creative partner — not just a shortcut? The vision must be human. The cultural knowledge must be human. The judgment must be human.
A designer collaborating with generative AI on a culturally authentic campaign.
DIMENSION 06
Future of AI
Does the student understand how AI evolved — from rule-based to ML to Transformers to RAG to Agentic AI — and the four questions that evaluate any AI system?
A policymaker shaping the regulations that govern AI at national scale.

PARTNER
SCHOOL.

FutureMind is open to every school in India — CBSE, ICSE, IB, Cambridge, or State Board. AI thinking skills are not a curriculum requirement for one board. They are a life requirement for every student, regardless of which syllabus they follow.

For CBSE schools implementing the mandatory CT&AI curriculum from 2026–27, FutureMind is the assessment and competition layer that takes students from knowledge to applied judgment. For ICSE schools whose students study AI technically, FutureMind tests the critical thinking side that the technical subject does not cover. For IB and Cambridge schools, FutureMind provides the structured national AI literacy competition that no existing curriculum yet offers on a national stage.

One coordinator. Minimal administration. Maximum association value. The school whose students perform best nationally will be the one everyone knows pioneered AI thinking skills.
