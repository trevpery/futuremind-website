DIMENSION 02
AI Safety & Ethics
Does the student understand responsibility, privacy and the boundaries of ethical AI use? When AI causes harm, someone must be accountable — and understanding that is a critical life skill.
A future lawyer understanding AI evidence admissibility and data privacy in court.
03
DIMENSION 03
Spotting Hallucinations & Bias
Can the student identify when AI is wrong, misleading or systematically unfair? Confidence in AI output is not the same as accuracy — and the difference matters enormously.
A future doctor questioning an AI diagnostic output before it becomes a treatment decision.
04
DIMENSION 04
AI in the Real World
Does the student see how AI is already reshaping industries and jobs? The seven types of AI — and the specific type that is changing their chosen field right now, today.
A future farmer understanding AI-driven crop prediction, pest detection, and market pricing tools.
05
DIMENSION 05
Creative Use of AI
Can the student use AI as a genuine creative partner — not just a shortcut? The vision must be human. The cultural knowledge must be human. The judgment must be human. AI amplifies all three.

Does the student understand how AI evolved, what types exist beyond ChatGPT, and where it is heading — RAG systems, agentic AI, and the four questions that evaluate any AI system.
